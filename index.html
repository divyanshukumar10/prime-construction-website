Our Services

Engineered Flooring Systems for Performance, Precision & Reliability

We provide advanced industrial and commercial flooring solutions engineered for strength, precision, and long-term performance. Our services combine proven technology, international standards, and expert execution to meet the most demanding project requirements.

SFRC Flooring (Steel Fiber Reinforced Concrete)

SFRC flooring is a high-performance concrete solution designed for heavy loads, dynamic traffic, and long service life.

Key Benefits:

  • High load-bearing capacity with optimized slab thickness
  • Superior crack control and durability
  • Reduced need for conventional reinforcement
  • Faster construction and fewer joints
  • Designed in accordance with TR34 and international standards

Applications:

Warehouses, logistics centers, factories, heavy-duty pavements, and high-rack storage facilities.

Super Flat Flooring (DM1, FM1, FM2)

Super Flat floors are engineered to achieve precise flatness and level tolerances essential for modern warehousing and high-reach material handling systems.

Key Benefits:

  • High flatness and level accuracy (DM1, FM1, FM2)
  • Smooth, durable surface for efficient operations
  • Reduced vibration and improved safety for MHE
  • Suitable for narrow aisle and high-rack warehouses

Applications:

Logistics hubs, distribution centers, automated warehouses, and industrial storage facilities.

Traditional Reinforced Concrete Slabs

Traditional reinforced slabs remain a reliable and cost-effective solution for many structural and industrial applications.

Key Benefits:

  • Reinforced with rebar or mesh as per design requirements
  • Suitable for moderate load conditions
  • Flexible design options
  • Proven construction methodology

Applications:

Industrial buildings, commercial structures, external slabs, and general-purpose flooring.

Epoxy Flooring Systems

Epoxy flooring provides a seamless, durable, and chemical-resistant surface ideal for industrial and commercial environments.

Key Benefits:

  • Seamless and dust-free finish
  • High resistance to chemicals, oils, and stains
  • Abrasion and impact resistant
  • Easy to clean and maintain
  • Anti-Slip and decorative options available

Applications:

Factories, warehouses, parking areas, laboratories, food-processing units, and commercial spaces.

PU Flooring (Polyurethane Flooring)

PU flooring offers superior flexibility, thermal resistance, and hygiene, making it ideal for demanding environments.

Key Benefits:

  • Excellent resistance to thermal shock
  • High chemical and moisture resistance
  • Seamless and hygienic surface
  • Durable, flexible, and comfortable underfoot
  • Anti-slip and food-grade options available

Applications:

Food & beverage plants, cold rooms, pharmaceutical facilities, commercial kitchens, and wet processing areas.
