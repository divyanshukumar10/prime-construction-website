Our Process

A Structured Approach from Assessment to Handover.

  1. 01 · Site Assessment

    Detailed evaluation of your facility, substrate condition, operating loads, and environmental factors.

  2. 02 · Design Proposal

    We recommend the most suitable flooring system with technical specifications, build-ups, and timelines.

  3. 03 · Surface Preparation

    Diamond grinding, shot blasting, and repair works to ensure optimal adhesion and long-term performance.

  4. 04 · Installation & Coating

    Our certified team executes installation using industry-standard methods, materials, and equipment.

  5. 05 · Post-Project Support

    We provide maintenance guidelines, inspection support, and repair strategies to protect your flooring investment.
